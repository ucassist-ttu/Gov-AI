As a SNAP recipient, you can also take advantage of the resources below.

Suicide and Crisis Lifeline

Call or Text: 1-844-482-4636

Connects you to trained counselors who provide 24/7, free, and confidential support for anyone experiencing thoughts of suicide or emotional distress.

Crisis Text Line – Text HOME to

741741

Free, 24/7 text-based mental health support from trained crisis counselors.

Grief Support Line –

1-800-395-5757

Offers compassionate listening and support for individuals coping with loss and grief.

Self-Harm Hotline –

1-800-366-8288

Provides confidential support and resources for those struggling with self-injury or thoughts of self-harm.

Domestic Violence Hotline –

1-800-799-7233

Offers 24-hour confidential support, safety planning, and resources for anyone affected by domestic violence.

Child Protective Services (CPS) –

1-877-237-0004

Report suspected child abuse or neglect; available 24/7.

Adult Protective Services –

1-888-277-8366

Report abuse, neglect, or exploitation of vulnerable or older adults in Tennessee.

Elder Abuse Hotline –

1-800-252-8966

Report elder abuse, neglect, or exploitation; available 24/7.

National Child Abuse Hotline –

1-800-422-4453

Provides crisis intervention, information, and resources for children at risk or adults reporting suspected child abuse.

TN Redline –

1-800-889-9789

A 24/7 referral service for Tennesseans seeking information and treatment options for substance use disorders.

Addiction Helpline (SAMHSA National Helpline) –

1-800-662-4357

Free, confidential treatment referral and information service for individuals and families facing substance use or mental health issues.

Alcoholics Anonymous (Nashville Area) –

1-615-831-1050

Connects callers with local AA meetings and peer support for recovery from alcohol use disorder.

National HIV/AIDS Hotline –

1-800-232-4636

Provides information about HIV testing, prevention, treatment, and local health resources.

Eating Disorder Helpline –

1-800-931-2237

Connects callers to trained volunteers who provide information, referrals, and emotional support for eating disorder concerns.

Poison Control –

1-800-222-1222

Offers immediate, expert assistance for poison emergencies or exposure questions (available nationwide, 24/7).

Youth Crisis Hotline –

1-800-448-4663

Provides 24-hour support for teens and young adults dealing with family conflict, depression, or other crises.

Veterans Crisis Line –

Dial 988, then Press 1 or Text 838255

Connects veterans and their families with trained responders for confidential crisis support, available 24/7.
