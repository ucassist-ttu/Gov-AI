As a SNAP recipient, you can also take advantage of the following resources:

TruConnect Lifeline Plan

  • Free unlimited talk, text, and international calling
  • 4.5GB of data
  • a free SIM card

Assurance Wireless Cell Phone Plan

  • Free monthly calls, texts, and data,
  • a free smartphone
  • $0 cell phone bill and no annual contract
  • Eligibility based on household income or participation in SSI, SNAP, or Medicaid

Cricket Wirelessn

  • $15/month cell phone plan
  • unlimited talk and text
  • 1GB of high-speed data

AT&T Access

  • Low-cost wireless home internet service for households with at least one SNAP participant
  • Includes free installation
  • In-home Wi-Fi
  • No annual contract
  • Costs $10/month or less depending on available speed

Comcast Internet Essentials

  • Internet service as low as $9.95 per month

Spectrum Internet Assistance

  • Low-income internet option for households participating in NSLP, CEP for NSLP, or SSI (age 65+)

Lifeline Program

  • Federal program reducing monthly phone or internet costs for households at or below 135% of the federal poverty level
  • Available for those receiving SNAP, Medicaid, SSI, public housing, or Veterans Pension and Survivors Benefits

Low Income Home Energy Assistance Program (LIHEAP)

  • Federally funded grant program helping low-income households meet immediate energy needs
  • Eligibility: income at or below 150% of U.S. Poverty Guidelines

Weatherization Assistance Program (WAP)

  • Provides home energy improvements such as insulation, caulking, storm windows, and minor repairs
  • Improves efficiency and reduces utility costs

Children’s Health Insurance Program (CHIP)

  • Provides low-cost or free health coverage for children under 19 in families with low to moderate income

Amazon Prime

  • $5.99 per month with a valid EBT or Medicaid card

Walmart+

  • $6.47 per month or $49 per year with qualifying benefits

Nashville Zoo

  • $50 annual membership

Adventure Science Center

  • Discounted admission (check site for details)

Huntsville Space Center

  • Discounted admission (check site for details)

Nashville Farmers’ Market

  • SNAP participants receive $1 in Fresh Bucks tokens for every $1 spent using an EBT card

Seeds and Plants

  • SNAP benefits can be used to purchase seeds and plants at any retailer that accepts SNAP

Lifeline Program (Other Assistance)

  • Reduces monthly phone or internet costs for eligible households
