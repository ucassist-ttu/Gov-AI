Explore services offered by the Upper Cumberland Development District

Public Transit

UC Public Transit provides rides throughout the entire Upper Cumberland region and beyond.

Call to book a ride at 1-833-UC Trips (1-833-828-7477)

Or visit them at UCPublicTransit.com

Seniors & Disabled Persons

The Upper Cumberland Area Agency on Aging and Disability has many programs to help the senior and disabled population of the region. Meals, in-home services, Medicare aid, and much more are available.

Contact them at 1-866-836-6678

Or visit them at ucdd.org/aaad

Assistance For Families

With a region-wide mission to lift 1,600 children and their families out of poverty by 2025, Empower Upper Cumberland (Empower UC) offers programs to aid those in need throughout the Upper Cumberland.

Call them at 1-833-EMPWRUC (1-833-367-9782)

Or visit them at EmpowerUpperCumberland.org

Services For Relative Caregivers

Relative Caregiver provides support and aid to relatives who find themselves taking care of children.

Call them at (931)432-4111 to see how they can help.

Medicare Assistance

The SHIP, State Health Insurance Program, provides free and objective one-on-one counseling and education to individuals and groups in regards to Medicare and other related insurances (such as Part D).

Call them for services at 1-877-801-0044

Or visit them at tnmedicarehelp.com

Your Local UCHRA

Commodities, homemaker services, recovery support, and drivers education are just a few of the family oriented services that they offer.

Call (931)528-1127 for more info

Or visit them at UCHRA.com or any of the fourteen county offices

Housing For Seniors and Disabled Persons

UCDD’s House and Home programs offer emergency home repairs, pre-packaged mortgages, repair loans, and apartment rentals for elderly and disabled.

Call 1-931-432-4111 for more info

Or visit them at UCDD.org
